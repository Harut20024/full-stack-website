Game Of Life

"Game Of Life is an interactive simulation that allows you to observe the natural behaviors of different objects as they grow and interact within the game world. Unlike traditional games, you won't need to control the actions; instead, the game's characters follow their own rules and adapt to their environment. Experience the evolution of life, from basic grass to predators and even fire, in this dynamic and autonomous simulation."
